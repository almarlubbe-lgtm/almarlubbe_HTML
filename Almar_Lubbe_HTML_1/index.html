Fun Fact: The cast underwent a rigorous boot camp to prepare for their roles, and the Tiger tank used in the film is one of only a few operational Tiger I tanks in the world!

About the Film

Fury is a gripping World War II film that follows a battle-hardened U.S. Army sergeant named Wardaddy as he commands a Sherman tank and her five-man crew on a deadly mission behind enemy lines. The film portrays the brutal reality of war with unflinching honesty, showing the moral complexities and psychological toll of combat.

Set in April 1945, as the Allies make their final push into Nazi Germany, the crew of the tank Fury must confront overwhelming odds and face their own mortality. The story explores themes of brotherhood, sacrifice, and the cost of survival in the final days of the war.
